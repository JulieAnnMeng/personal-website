About

After graduating the University of Centeral Florida, Julie began working as an Event Coordinator. She planned many public events, both small and large in scale. After several years, she found herself wanting to do something more with her life, dealing with upset event guests was not as fulfilling as she had hoped. She quit her job and began her search for a new career.

Career Change

A friend of her's completed the Software Engineering cohort at Flatiron school, and was excited to inform Julie of her new career. Julie then applied for the program and was ecstatic that she was accepted. Julie will be attending the Software Engineering course at Flatiron School. She is set to join the programs cohort on August 9, 2021. Ready for a career change and to learn something new, Julie is up to the challenge!
